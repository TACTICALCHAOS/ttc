Whiteout Survival

Whiteout Survival is a survival strategy game centered on a glacial apocalypse theme. Fascinating mechanics and intricate details await you to explore! The catastrophic decline in global temperatures has wreaked havoc on human society.

(Alliance) Tactical Chaos

Welcome to Tactical Chaos in state #642!

Ranked 8th in power and alliance kills, we're a highly active alliance with a few rules:

  1. Do not attack allies or top 15 alliances.
  2. Be active daily in chat and events.
  3. 3-day inactivity leads to demotion or removal.
  4. Bullying or harassment isn't tolerated.
